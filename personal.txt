  Then every time you want to analyze a prop:

  source .venv/bin/activate  

  python analyze.py

  It'll prompt you for 4 things:
  Player name: Luke Kennard
  Prop line (e.g. '20+ points'): 10.5+ points
  American odds (e.g. -166 or +120): -280
  Tonight's opponent: Houston Rockets

  Or skip the prompts with one line:
  python analyze.py --player "Luke Kennard" --line "10.5+ points" --odds -280 --opponent "Houston Rockets"

  That's it. Takes about 10-15 seconds to pull live data then prints the full breakdown.
